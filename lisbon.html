PE2025 Getting around

Lisbon has a vast interconnected network of buses, subway and train. Each requires a different ticket but you have the option to pay with a contactless card or buy a rechargeable transport card that works across all public transports. If you are planning multiple trips for the same day, consider buying a 1 day ticket which allows unlimited trips during 24h. For more information, check out this page.
