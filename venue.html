Conference Venue IIT Headquarters

The Conference will take place in the headquarters of IIT, in Via Morego 30, on Monday, Wednesday and Thursday. A shuttle will bring the Attendees from the City center to IIT's headquarters, and back, in these days. On Tuesday 27th, the Conference will be held in the Doge's Palace.
