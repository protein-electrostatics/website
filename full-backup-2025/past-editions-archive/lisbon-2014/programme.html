Programme


This meeting is aimed at experimentalists, theoreticians and simulator studying the molecular-level mechanisms behind electrostatic effects in proteins and other biomolecules. Being a small specialized meeting, the purpose is to keep an informal atmosphere of frank discussion – the goal is to address the problems, not to avoid them. The meeting is primarily aimed at PIs, all of whom should have the opportunity to give a talk. However, we also welcome highly motivated students and post-docs working in this field, who should bring a poster (eventually promoted to a short talk).

The meeting will consist of talks and poster sessions taking place in the morning and afternoon. The detailed schedule will depend on the number/type of participants and will be available when enough information is available (see also current list of participants).
