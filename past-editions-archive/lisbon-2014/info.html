Venue

The campus of the Faculty of Sciences is located on the west side of Lisbon, at the northern end of the Garden of Campo Grande. Sessions will take place inside the C1 building, on the 3rd floor.

Address

Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade de Lisboa, Campo Grande, 1749-016 Lisboa, Portugal

Arriving

The venue is located 3.5 km from Lisbon Airport. There are metro and bus connections.

- Metro: From "Aeroporto" station (Red line) you can travel to "Campo Grande" station (Yellow and Green line) or to "Cidade Universitária" station (Yellow line) which are the nearest stations to the Faculty of Sciences. The single ticket price for one journey is € 1.40 (+ € 0.50 reusable card). For further information visit Metro de Lisboa.

- Bus: From "Aeroporto" stop you can travel to "Campo Grande/Avenida do Brasil" stop which is the nearest to the Faculty of Sciences. There are three bus connections: 731, 750 and 783. The single ticket price for one journey is € 1.80 (board ticket). For further Bus information please visit Carris.
